Logo Usage Guidelines

✓ Do
  • Use on white or light-gray backgrounds
  • Use the reversed version on dark or navy backgrounds
  • Maintain the two-color wordmark treatment (navy + blue)
  • Scale proportionally at all sizes
  • Use monochrome version when printing in single color
✗ Don't
  • Stretch, skew, or distort the logo
  • Change the logo colors outside approved palette
  • Place on busy photographic backgrounds without overlay
  • Recreate the logo in other typefaces
  • Add drop shadows or effects to the logo
Section 02
Logo Clearspace

Always maintain a minimum clearspace around the logo equal to the height of the letter "O" in the wordmark (designated as 1x). This ensures the logo is never visually crowded by surrounding elements.

Section 05
Iconography Style

Icons should be outline style with consistent 1.5–2px stroke weight. Rounded line caps and joins. Avoid filled or flat icon sets — the open, airy outline style reinforces the aviation and premium feel of the brand.

✓ Do
Use outline-style icons with consistent 1.5–2px stroke weight. Keep all icons from the same family for visual cohesion. Use brand navy (#0D2B6E) as the primary icon color, or sky blue (#4CB8E0) for accent icons.
✗ Don't
Mix outline and filled icon styles. Use overly detailed or decorative icons that don't match the brand's clean, professional tone. Avoid icons with drop shadows, multi-color fills, or gradients.
